We are always looking for talented people to join our team

Huawei's London AI Research Center is part of the wider Noah's Ark Laboratory, with offices in Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Beijing, Toronto, London, Paris, among others. Research areas of the lab mainly include machine learning, data mining, computer vision, speech and language processing, information and knowledge management, internet of things, and intelligent devices.

Tower Bridge at Dawn, by George Johnson (2013), CC BY-SA 3.0

If you would like to work with us in the Enhanced Imaging Group (London Research Centre) and conduct research towards AI-driven innovative camera systems we are always looking for outstanding candidates, and we generally have the following open positions available:

  • Research Associate: After completion of the PhD program, similar to a Postdoctoral position in mainland Europe.
  • Research Intern: You should be enrolled in a PhD program already, and duration is typically 6 months. You will be working with a Research Scientist in one of our research projects, ideally writing up a paper submission as part of your work.

Applicants should have:

  • Strong mathematical background
  • Strong programming skills (Python, tensorflow, pytorch)
  • Publications in relevant conferences (e.g. CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, Siggraph, NeurIPS, ICML)
  • Knowledge/experience in computer vision
  • Knowledge/experience in deep learning
  • Knowledge/experience working within the neural rendering and computational photography domain (e.g. denoising, HDR imaging, RAW processing) is desired but not a must
  • Strong interest in our research areas (3d photography, intelligent imaging, HDR imaging)

If you would like to get more information about these openings or ultimately start the application process please contact me via email () and include a CV with a full publication list when relevant.
